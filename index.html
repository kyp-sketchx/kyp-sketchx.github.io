About Me:

I received my PhD at Queen Mary University of London, and during which I spent some wonderful time at SketchX , CVSSP, University of Surrey. My PhD advisors are Dr. Yi-Zhe Song and Prof. Tao Xiang. I also work closely with Dr. Timothy Hospedales from the University of Edinburgh. Before that, I received the first-class B.S. degree from the Sino-UK Joint Programme held between Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications and Queen Mary University of London in July 2016. I delivered the valedictory speech to the class of 2016.
