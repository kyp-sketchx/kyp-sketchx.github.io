About Me:

I am a fourth year Ph.D candidate at Queen Mary University of London, and affiliated to SketchX Research Lab , CVSSP, University of Surrey. My supervisors are Dr. Yi-Zhe Song and Prof. Tao Xiang. I also work closely with Dr. Timothy Hospedales from the University of Edinburgh. Before that, I received the first-class B.S. degree from the Sino-UK Joint Programme held between Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications and Queen Mary University of London in July 2016. I'm honoured to deliver the valedictory speech to the class of 2016.
